About

Ad astra per aspera.

  • My name is Lisa Raisa Ferrari.

    I am an artist that uses the medium of photography to translate the images from my mind to the outer world. Doing so I look beyond everyday life. My art represents this.

  • Juli 2013

    I received my Bachelor of Design (Photography) at the Willem de Kooning Art Academy in Rotterdam, the Netherlands.

  • August 2015

    I received my Master of Arts at Leiden University, the Netherlands. My thesis revolved around undercover photographs and videos shot at factory farms.
